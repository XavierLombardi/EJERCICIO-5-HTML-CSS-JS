A pottery studio dedicated to creativity and community.


Sarah Glass Ceramics is a woman-owned pottery studio located in Fort Lauderdale. Our mission is to provide everyone of all skill levels a strong foundation and supportive environment to experience the relaxing art of pottery making.

We are happy to provide studio space and equipment to let you channel your inner artist. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, we have the right tools and guidance to help you create something beautiful.
